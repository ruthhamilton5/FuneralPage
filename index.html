For almost 100 years now Hamilton's Funeral Service have served the surrounding area.

We are proud to say that we are one of the oldest established funeral undertaking firms in South Derry.

The business is presently under the management of Samuel Hamilton, grandson of the founder. Because we are a small firm we believe that in your time of need we can offer a personal and caring service from your first enquiry.
